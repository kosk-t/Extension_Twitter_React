Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-13

Twitter Reaction Counter runs entirely in your browser. It does not collect, send, or sell personal data.

Data Processed

The extension reads the current Twitter/X page to calculate engagements and shows the results locally. No data is sent to our servers.

Storage

Extension preferences may be saved using chrome.storage.sync. You can clear them via your browser settings or by removing the extension.

Permissions

The extension requests only the activeTab permission to run on the tab you use it on.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page.
